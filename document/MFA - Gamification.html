When the ship is days from help, your first minutes matter.

This gamified STCW Medical First Aid course prepares designated officers to take immediate effective action for illness and injury onboard, make structured assessments, give first aid with shipboard resources, communicate with radio medical advice, and prepare records and evacuation support.
