Stress Management Onboard — Staying Effective Under Pressure

Practical offline course for all ranks on recognising stress, staying effective under pressure, communicating clearly, managing fatigue and seeking help early.

RED — STOPStress or fatigue is affecting safety, judgement, mood, sleep or watchkeeping.
YELLOW — PAUSEBreathe, check facts, reduce overload, clarify instructions and ask for support.
GREEN — RESPONDCommunicate clearly, act by priority, record concerns and support the team.
