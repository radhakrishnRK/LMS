Housekeeping and Vessel Standards — Pride in the Ship

Practical offline course for all ranks and junior officers on maintaining vessel standards, clear escape routes, safe storage, fire-load control, hygiene, welfare and inspection readiness.

RED — STOPBlocked escape route, fire load, loose gear, unlabelled chemical or unsafe spill.
YELLOW — VERIFYCheck ownership, labels, securing, segregation, access and follow-up required.
GREEN — MAINTAINClean, clear, labelled, secured and recorded where required.
