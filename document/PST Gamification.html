The body loses heat fastest from specific areas. Tap all 4 zones on the diagram to discover where — and why the HELP position protects them.
Found: 0 / 4 zones
Why HELP works
Arms crossed over chest protect armpits. Knees pulled up protect the groin. Head kept up above water. These 4 zones account for most body heat loss. HELP reduces heat loss by up to 50% vs treading water.
