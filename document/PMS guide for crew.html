Planned Maintenance System (PMS) — User Guide for Crew

Practical offline course for engineers, officers and bosuns on using PMS correctly, managing overdue tasks, signing off honestly, raising defects and supporting audit readiness.

RED — STOPFalse sign-off, hidden overdue critical tasks, unsafe deferral or unreported defects.
YELLOW — VERIFYCheck interval, job scope, spares, manual, risk control and authorisation.
GREEN — RECORDComplete the work properly, record evidence and raise follow-up when required.
