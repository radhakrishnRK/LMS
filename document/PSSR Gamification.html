From “rules on paper” to shipboard behaviour.

This gamified PSSR course is designed for new seafarers before sea service. It turns STCW A‑VI/1‑4 into practical shipboard decisions: emergencies, pollution prevention, safe work, communication, human relations, fatigue control, and prevention of violence and harassment onboard.
